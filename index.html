Meet Our Visionary Leader

Alh. Musbahu Bala Dauda

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in international trade and logistics, Alh Musbahu Bala founded Balad NG with a revolutionary vision to transform global commerce. His expertise spans across supply chain management, international regulations, and market expansion strategies.

Under Alh Musbahu's leadership, Balad NG has expanded to over 50 countries and established partnerships with major logistics providers worldwide. His commitment to innovation and customer-centric solutions has positioned the company as an industry leader.

"Our mission is to break down barriers in global trade, making it accessible and efficient for businesses of all sizes. We believe that seamless international commerce drives economic growth and creates opportunities worldwide."
