About Balad NG

Discover our journey, mission, and the team behind our revolutionary global trade solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Balad NG emerged from a vision to revolutionize global trade by making it more accessible, efficient, and transparent for businesses of all sizes. What started as a small import-export consultancy has grown into a global logistics powerhouse.

Our journey began when our founder, Alh Musbahu, identified significant inefficiencies in international trade processes. He envisioned a company that would leverage technology and expertise to streamline these processes, creating a seamless experience for clients worldwide.

Today, Balad NG operates in over 50 countries, with a network of strategic partners and state-of-the-art facilities. Our commitment to innovation and customer-centric solutions has positioned us as a leader in the global logistics industry.
